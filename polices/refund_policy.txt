Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund for eligible products within 30 calendar days from the date of purchase.

To be eligible for a refund, the product must be unused and in its original condition.

Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days after the refund request is approved.

Digital products, downloadable content, and subscription fees are non-refundable.

Refund requests submitted after 30 days from the purchase date will not be accepted.

In case of defective or damaged products, customers must report the issue within 7 days of delivery to be eligible for a refund or replacement.
